A comforting Filipino classic with tender chicken simmered in sweet pineapple goodness, best enjoyed with hot steamed rice and shared with family.

* ⏲️ Prep time: 20 min.
* 🍳 Cook time: 45 min.
* 🍽️ Servings: 5

## Ingredients

* 1 lb chicken, cut into serving pieces
* 1 cup pineapple chunks
* 1 cup pineapple juice
* 2 chopped carrots
* 1 red bell pepper, sliced
* 1 chopped onion
* 3 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 cup evaporated milk (optional for creamy version)
* 2 tbsp fish sauce
* Salt and pepper to taste
* 2 tbsp cooking oil

## Directions

1. Heat cooking oil in a pan and sauté onions and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
3. Pour fish sauce and stir for a minute.
4. Add pineapple juice and let it simmer for about 15–20 minutes until chicken becomes tender.
5. Add carrots and pineapple chunks, then continue cooking until vegetables soften.
6. Pour evaporated milk for a creamy texture (optional).
7. Add bell peppers and season with salt and pepper according to taste.
8. Simmer for another 3–5 minutes and serve hot.

## Notes

✨ For a richer flavor, let the chicken absorb the pineapple juice longer before adding milk.

🍚 Best served with warm rice and an ice-cold drink.
